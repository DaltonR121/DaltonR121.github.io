ABOUT ME

Welcome! My name is Ryan Dalton and I'm a Full Stack Software Engineer currently living in New Jersey!

I attended a "bootcamp" (if you will) at 19 years old. It wasn't to code - it was to drive tractor-trailer! The program was 3-months long and I came out with a Class A CDL. I spent the next 10 years of my adult working life as a Truck Driver. I enjoyed it and was successful doing it but I always felt pulled towards something else.

Over the years, I had always dabbled with the idea of web development. I even got my hands on Adobe Dreamweaver late in my teenage years and thought that's what web development was today. I believe it was 2018 when I found Codecademy and took a deep dive into JavaScript. Even after gaining a much better understanding of what modern web development looks like, I still didn't see a clear path for someone like me to get into the tech space.

Late-2020, it was time to get out of my comfort zone and make a change. I had read the success stories of others that had come from unrelated fields into Software Engineering and I was determined to make my story one of them. I discovered App Academy and went to a "bootcamp" for the second time! I graduated from App Academy in July of 2021 and am more driven than ever to keep growing, learning, and gaining experience. I am comfortable working with technologies such as JavaScript, ReactJS, Redux, Express, Python, Flask, Postgres, SQLAlchemy, and Sequelize. I am confident in my ability to learn at an accelerated pace and can't wait to jump into projects with real-world intent!

PORTFOLIO

Financial Friendship

According to Charles Schwab's 2019 Modern Wealth Index Survey, about 59% of adults in the U.S. admit to living paycheck to paycheck. What is the best way to take control of your finances? Be INVOLVED in your finances! Financial Friendship is an application designed with the idea of manually updating the information here weekly or even daily! Building a habit of knowing what is going on with your money is the best way to keep more of it!

The idea for Financial Friendship came from a spreadsheet I actually keep for my own family's finances. It occurred to me that the more often I updated that spreadsheet, the more in-tune I felt with our finances. This application is essentially modeled after that spreadsheet!

Technologies Used:

JavaScript, CSS, React/Redux, Python, Flask, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy, Chart.js

spaceXchange

The galactic empire is being developed as we speak, quickly transforming uninhabitable wastelands into industrial giants, and making a ton of money along the way. spaceXchange is your one stop shop for trading your USD into any other planet's currency, allowing you to take part in the universal stock market.

spaceXchange is a Robinhood clone, created to allow a space-faring civilization to participate in the universal stock market.

Navigamer

Navigamer is designed to help gamers keep track of which games they've already played, which games they have yet to play, and read reviews from other passionate gamers to learn more before starting a new game. Supporting nearly every console ever made, navigating through your immense game-library can be made easy with a few simple clicks. Don't see your favorite game? No worries! We're constantly growing thanks to over a trillion users around the world, and after signing up you can add any game that we might be missing.

Navigamer is a Goodreads clone, created with the goal of providing gamers with a place to keep track of games they've played, as well as browse for new games to play.

Technologies Used:

JavaScript, React/Redux, Python, Flask, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy
