ABOUT ME

Welcome! My name is Ryan Dalton and I'm a Full Stack Software Engineer currently living in New Jersey!

I attended a "bootcamp" (if you will) at 19 years old. It wasn't to code - it was to drive tractor-trailer! The program was 3-months long and I came out with a Class A CDL. I spent the next 10 years of my adult working life as a Truck Driver. I enjoyed it and was successful doing it but I always felt pulled towards something else.

Over the years, I had always dabbled with the idea of web development. I even got my hands on Adobe Dreamweaver late in my teenage years and thought that's what web development was today. I believe it was 2018 when I found Codecademy and took a deep dive into JavaScript. Even after gaining a much better understanding of what modern web development looks like, I still didn't see a clear path for someone like me to get into the tech space.

Late-2020, it was time to get out of my comfort zone and make a change. I had read the success stories of others that had come from unrelated fields into Software Engineering and I was determined to make my story one of them. I discovered App Academy and went to a "bootcamp" for the second time! I graduated from App Academy in July of 2021 and am more driven than ever to keep growing, learning, and gaining experience. I am comfortable working with technologies such as JavaScript, ReactJS, Redux, Express, Python, Flask, Postgres, SQLAlchemy, and Sequelize. I am confident in my ability to learn at an accelerated pace and can't wait to jump into projects with real-world intent!
